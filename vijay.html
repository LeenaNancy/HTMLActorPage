Vijay

Vijay was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu.[15] His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a Tamil film director[16] and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.[17] His father is of Christian Vellalar descent and his mother is a Hindu.[18][19] Vijay was baptised at the age of 12.
